Job Title: Senior Machine Learning Engineer
Company: TechVision AI
Location: San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI Platform team. 
You will design, build, and deploy ML systems at scale that power our core product features.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement machine learning models for production use cases including NLP and recommendation systems
- Build end-to-end ML pipelines from data ingestion to model deployment and monitoring
- Collaborate with research scientists to productionize models from research to production
- Optimize model performance, latency, and throughput for large-scale serving
- Lead technical design reviews and mentor junior engineers
- Define ML platform standards and best practices for the team

Required Qualifications:
- 5+ years of experience in machine learning engineering
- Strong Python programming skills
- Proficiency with deep learning frameworks: PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Experience with MLOps tools: MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar
- Experience deploying models to cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Solid understanding of NLP, recommendation systems, or computer vision
- Experience with distributed training and model optimization

Preferred Qualifications:
- M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field
- Experience with LLMs and transformer architectures
- Publications in top ML venues (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, etc.)
- Experience with Kubernetes and Docker for ML workloads

Compensation: $200,000 - $280,000 base + equity
